The sky shifted from a pale amber to a deep indigo as the sun dipped behind the mountains, casting long shadows over the quiet valley. Birds chirped their final songs of the day, while a gentle breeze stirred the leaves, carrying the scent of pine and fresh earth. In the distance, the faint rustle of a river could be heard, winding its way through the forest like a silver ribbon. It was one of those rare evenings where time seemed to slow down, urging everything and everyone to simply breathe and be.
